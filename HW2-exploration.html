  • 1. What have you learned about your data? Have any potentially interesting patterns emerged? (5-8 sentences)

I found it interesting how much of the data was unknown, either not recorded in in the dataset or recorded as unknown/unconfirmed/etc in the dataset. It makes sense since the data was collected and compiled from various news sources, NGO’s, and other sources from multiple different languages, and its reasonable that some of that data would either be lost in translation or not recorded at all. I also found the gender distribution interesting, where I found that the majority of victims seem to be men. I’m unsure if this is due to the personal characteristics of the environmental defender, or if other social factors could be the cause, though. I also found it interesting that many identified perpetrators seem to be landowners. I’m curious to know if the landowners are corporate or if they’re people unaffiliated with a business/corporation.
